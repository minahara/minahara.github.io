About Me

I am in my senior year at EWU, majoring in Visual Communication Design. Growing up, I spent half my time in Spokane, WA and the other half in Hayden, ID. Creating has always been a passion of mine but I didn't know how I would I apply this passion to a career until someone told me to check out the VCD program. And now I am more excited than ever to continue living my dream of creating.
